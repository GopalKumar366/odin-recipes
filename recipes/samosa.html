Samosa

Description

Samosas are one of the most beloved snacks across the globe, known for their crispy exterior and flavorful filling. These golden, triangular pastries are a staple in Indian cuisine but have gained popularity worldwide for their irresistible taste and versatility.

Ingredients

  • Maida
  • Potatoes
  • oil

Steps

  2. Prepare the Filling
  3. Shape the Samosa
  4. Fry the Samosas
  5. Serve
