Jalebi

Description

Jalebi is a popular Indian sweet known for its bright orange, spiral shape and irresistible sweetness. Made from a simple fermented batter, jalebis are deep-fried to crispy perfection and then soaked in a fragrant sugar syrup, creating a delightful contrast of textures. With their crunchy exterior and juicy, syrupy center, jalebis are a favorite at festivals, celebrations, and as a indulgent treat any time of the day. Serve them warm for the ultimate melt-in-your-mouth experience!

Ingredients

  • Maida
  • Sugar
  • Yogurt

Steps

  1. Prepare the Batter
  2. Make the sugar syrup
  3. Fry the Jalebi
  4. Soak in Sugar syrup
  5. Serve and Enjoy
